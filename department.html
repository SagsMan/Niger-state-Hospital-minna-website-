Award winning patient care

At General Hospital Minna, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care that exceeds our patients' expectations. Our commitment to delivering high-quality, compassionate care has earned us numerous awards and recognition. Our team of skilled healthcare professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives the best possible treatment and support. We are proud to have received awards for our outstanding patient care, and we continue to strive for excellence in all that we do.

Opthomology

Clear Vision, Bright Future At General Hospital Minna, our Ophthalmology department is dedicated to helping you see the world clearly and beautifully. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists and optometrists diagnose and treat a range of eye conditions, from cataracts and glaucoma to age-related macular degeneration and conjunctivitis. We offer advanced treatments and surgeries, including LASIK and cataract removal, to help you achieve optimal vision and eye health.
